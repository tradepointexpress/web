About Us
About Us

Experts in Wellness and Personal Care

At Tradepoint Express, we specialize in the import and commercialization of wellness and personal care products, offering tailored solutions for pharmacies, hair salons and laboratories. From our headquarters in Caracas, Venezuela, we bring the best of the global market with a focus on quality and competitiveness. Founded by professionals from various disciplines at the Metropolitan University of Venezuela, our company manages imports within the country and has the capacity to sell under CIF terms abroad, also handling other Incoterms. We offer products in OPP bags, exclusive brands and a general brand, ensuring that our solutions are personalized and tailored to the needs of the market.

Mission and Vision

Mission

At Tradepoint Express, we are dedicated to importing and commercializing high-quality products, tailored to the needs of different types of businesses. We offer customized solutions for companies, ensuring exclusive, generic brand products and affordable options in OPP bags. We are committed to being a reliable partner for companies looking to import products from anywhere in the world, providing a comprehensive service with competitiveness, efficiency and transparency.

Vision

To be a leading company in the import of products in Latin America, offering our customers the best prices in the market. Our goal is to ensure that companies can place their products competitively, adapting to the needs of the market with flexibility in commercial terms. We distinguish ourselves by our transparency, efficiency and focus on providing solutions that drive the growth and success of our customers.
